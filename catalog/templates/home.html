Our Model

For All Counseling aims to increase access to mental health evaluations for people going through immigration and asylum proceedings despite language, location, or ability to pay. We also aim to pay clinicians a fair market rate to create a sustainable model where both clients and clinicians feel valued.We seek funding from donors and grants to offset the cost of our subsidized evaluations.
